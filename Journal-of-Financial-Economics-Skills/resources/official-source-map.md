# JFE Official Source Map

Sources used to make this pack specific to the **Journal of Financial Economics (JFE)**, published by Elsevier (North-Holland), ISSN **0304-405X**. All access dates: **2026-05-30**.

Where a target fact could not be confirmed from a primary source, the relevant skill marks it **UNVERIFIED** rather than guessing. None of this substitutes for the live submission instructions at submission time — the fee, portal, format rules, editor roster, and data policy all change.

## Editorial-leadership note

- **Editor-in-Chief: Toni M. Whited (University of Michigan), since 2021** (sources 3, 7).
- **Co-Editors: Dimitris Papanikolaou, Philipp Schnabl, Nikolai Roussanov** (source 3).
- **Founding Editor: Michael C. Jensen (1974)** (sources 3, 7).
- **G. William "Bill" Schwert** was the long-time **managing editor (1995–2021)** central to JFE's Rochester tradition; he is **not on the current masthead** (sources 3, 7). The pack therefore treats Schwert as historical context, not a current contact.
- Note: **Andrew Karolyi (Cornell)** is associated with the *Review of Financial Studies* (executive editor), **not** JFE's editor-in-chief — do not attribute him to JFE.

## UNVERIFIED items (deliberately not stated as fact in the skills)

- **Acceptance / desk-reject rate.** JFE does not publish a definitive current acceptance rate; the skills call it "very low, typical of top-three finance" and mark the precise figure UNVERIFIED.
- **Registered Reports track.** No JFE Registered Reports / two-stage-review track was found on the official JFE pages at access time; the skills do **not** claim one exists. (The earlier template's speculative "experimented with registered reports" line was removed.)
- **Exact current impact factor.** Reported by third parties as roughly high-single to low-double digits; no single official 2024 JCR value confirmed, so the skills do not quote a number.
