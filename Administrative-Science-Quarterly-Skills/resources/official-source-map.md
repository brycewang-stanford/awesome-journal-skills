# ASQ Official Source Map

Verified sources behind the ASQ-specific facts used across this skill pack.
Access date for all entries: **2026-05-30**. Volatile specifics (length norms,
abstract cap, named editors, acceptance rate, impact factor) change; re-verify on
the official pages before relying on them.

## Primary official source (verified verbatim from the live SAGE page)

URL: https://journals.sagepub.com/author-instructions/asq
(also mirrored as the SAGE guidelines PDF:
https://www.sagepub.com/docs/default-source/msg/submission-guidelines_-administrative-science-quarterly_-sage-journals.pdf)

Confirms, in the journal's own words:

- **Masthead motto:** "Dedicated to advancing the understanding of administration through empirical investigation and theoretical analysis."
- **Three editorial criteria:** does the research (1) advance understanding of organizing in contexts such as teams, enterprises, or markets; (2) develop a new theoretical account or empirical findings that challenge previous understandings; (3) address a significant and challenging problem of management?
- **Method neutrality:** "We do not attach greater significance to one methodological style than another, but we value data." Open to "qualitative or quantitative data collected from archives, the lab, or the field, as well as simulations and formal models." Theory-only papers considered but "have less often succeeded."
- **Reference style:** "As of January 2025, ASQ follows **APA style** for in-text citations and reference lists."
- **Length:** No firm page limit, but suggested **35–45 pages of text** (12-point Times New Roman, double spaced, 1-inch margins) plus references/tables/figures/appendices; "editors will unsubmit a very long manuscript and request cuts before considering it for review"; length-to-contribution ratio is an evaluation criterion.
- **Abstract:** "an informative abstract of **200 words or fewer**."
- **One key point:** "Each manuscript should contain one key point, which the author should be able to state in one sentence."
- **Submission:** ScholarOne at **https://mc.manuscriptcentral.com/asq**; upload **Word or PDF** (Word required if accepted, for production).
- **Blind review:** instructions to preserve the integrity of the **blind review** process; ScholarOne "no longer allows authors to list suggested reviewers" in the form.
- **Data & Methods Transparency** policy is mandatory; ASQ is a **COPE** member.
- **No resubmission** of papers ASQ previously rejected.
- **Keywords** chosen from the ASQ ScholarOne keyword list.
- **Impact Factor 6.5; 5-Year Impact Factor 13.9** (as shown on the SAGE page header).

## Fees — verified

- ASQ is a **hybrid** journal: **no submission fee and no mandatory publication fee**. Optional gold open access carries an article processing charge (**APC = US$3,000**), charged only *after* acceptance if the author opts in.
  - Source: SAGE OA / author-instructions for ASQ (https://journals.sagepub.com/author-instructions/asq).
  - NOTE: An earlier draft of this pack speculated ASQ "charges a submission fee." That was **incorrect** and has been removed. There is no submission fee.

## Acceptance rate / timeline (third-party — NOT an official ASQ figure)

- Reported **acceptance ~8%** and a mean time-to-first-decision in the low-40s of
  days appear on third-party trackers (editage, scirev). The journal does not post
  these officially — treat as approximate and verify.

## Impact factor caveat

- The live SAGE page header shows **IF 6.5 / 5-yr 13.9**; other third-party
  trackers report different values (e.g., ~10.4 for an earlier year). Cite the live
  SAGE page; treat trackers as approximate.
