# AMJ Manuscript Skeleton

> A structural template for a full empirical Academy of Management Journal (AMJ) article. Follow the current AOM Style Guide for citation/heading details and verify length limits on the official AMJ page. The anonymized manuscript must contain none of the identifying information shown on the title page.

## Anonymized Manuscript

### Abstract (within current AMJ word limit)

A single paragraph: the question and its theoretical motivation; the design and data; the key findings; the theoretical contribution. State what you do and what you find before significance.

**Keywords:** keyword 1; keyword 2; keyword 3; ...

- Phenomenon hook (what happens in the world)
- The theoretical conversation and the tension/problematization you address
- Research question (why/when)
- Preview of theoretical approach and study design
- Explicit statement of the intended contribution (2–4 sentences)
